Willkommen!

Welcome to the course website for Programming Practices for Research in Economics at the University of Zurich.

The goal of this course is to equip participants with a state of the art toolkit in computational practices within Economics and Business research. Learn more about the motivation and goals for the course here.

The 2018 Edition of Programming Practices for Research in Economics will be held from August 27, 2018 until September 19, 2018. You find the course website with the schedule and all other information here.
